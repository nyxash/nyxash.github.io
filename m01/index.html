About SG

Sarah Geronimo is one of the most iconic and versatile singers in the Philippines, known for her powerful voice, dynamic performances, and heartfelt music. She began her career at a young age and quickly rose to stardom, earning numerous awards and accolades both locally and internationally. With hit songs like "Tala" and "Kilometro," she has captured the hearts of millions and remains a beloved figure in the music industry. Beyond her music, Sarah is also an accomplished actress and a role model for aspiring artists.
