Batangas is the 9th largest province in the Philippines.

Batangas, located in southern Luzon just 2 to 3 hours from Metro Manila, is a captivating province known for its rich history, stunning landscapes, beautiful beaches, and picturesque coves. Surrounded by crystal-clear waters, it offers activities like scuba diving and snorkeling, showcasing a vibrant underwater ecosystem. The province is steeped in cultural heritage, featuring historical landmarks such as the Taal Basilica and the heritage town of Taal. Additionally, Batangas boasts scenic mountains and lush farmlands, making it perfect for outdoor activities.
